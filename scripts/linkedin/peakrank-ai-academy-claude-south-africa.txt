PeakRank sells visibility. But the biggest lever for most South African business owners isn't a better agency — it's learning to use AI themselves.

So I built the PeakRank AI Academy: a 3-tier curriculum teaching SA businesses to run on Anthropic's Claude.

I didn't want to invent folk wisdom about prompting, so it's grounded in Anthropic's own foundations:
→ the "4 Ds" framework — Delegation, Description, Discernment, Diligence
→ Claude for Small Business — connecting Claude to the tools you already use
→ an accuracy discipline: every module links the official source

The tiers:
→ Foundations — what Claude is, the 4 Ds, your first real prompts, POPIA-safe data habits
→ Growth Engine — repeatable marketing/content/admin workflows + a personal assistant
→ Accelerator — advanced prompting, custom agentic workflows, team rollout, a capstone

And POPIA runs through all of it from module one. Teaching AI without teaching data law would be reckless.

The agency makes you visible. The Academy makes you capable.
