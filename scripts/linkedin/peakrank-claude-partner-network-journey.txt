A build-in-public note.

I'm partway through taking PeakRank Digital down the Claude Partner Network path — and rather than wait for a finished story, here's the honest middle of it.

The path so far: application → initial review → approval to move forward → partner-portal onboarding → certifying the team. None of it is a rubber stamp; each stage expects real work before the next unlocks.

What I appreciate most: the programme rewards proof, not vibes. Standing is measured against concrete things — certified people, real production work, checkable references. You can't talk your way up. You show your work.

And to be accurate, not impressive: we're on the path, not at the summit. There's a real difference between doing the work toward a standard and having formally arrived. When we hit the next milestone, I'll share it properly.
